# Ed Mini

A minimal conversational voice agent focused on natural conversation quality. Mobile-first, designed for iPhone and iPad.

## What is Ed Mini?

Ed Mini is a lightweight voice agent that prioritizes what matters most: the quality of the conversation itself. Rather than packing in features, Ed Mini strips voice interaction down to its essentials — fast turn-taking, natural speech patterns, and a responsive feel that makes talking to an AI actually enjoyable.

## Design Principles

- **Conversation quality over feature count** — every design decision optimizes for how natural the interaction feels
- **Mobile-first** — built from the ground up for iPhone and iPad
- **Minimal latency** — real conversations don't have loading spinners
- **Voice-native** — not a text interface with speech bolted on

## Hackathon Origins
